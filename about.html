About Us

Appalachian Arts Sudio has evolved into a retreat for artists. Artists from all over come to use the studio space and to expand their creative endevours. Should you find yourself wanting to create bigger projects, and you lack studio space, please feel free to contact us, and let us welcome you. Take a tour of the sculpture studio, painting studio, carving studio, and print shop. Classes are also available, but with a twist. Studying here is not your typicl classroom setting, but rather a one-on-one learning exerience with one of the artists, depending on what you want to study.
